Fun Facts about dogs

Dogs have unique nose prints, sweat through their paws, and can smell your feelings. They are as smart as a two-year-old, can learn over 100 words, and even dream like humans.

Did you know?

The Basenji dog is known as the "barkless dog" because it makes a unique yodel-like sound instead of barking.
